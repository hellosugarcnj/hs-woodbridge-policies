Hello Sugar SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12 2026

This privacy policy applies to text messages sent by Hello Sugar Woodbridge.

Information we collect

When you contact us or book an appointment, we collect your name and mobile phone number.

How we use it

We use your mobile number to reply to messages you send us and to reply to a call we could not answer, with information related to your visit, such as confirming the door is unlocked and giving directions to the correct entrance.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile number or your messaging consent with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Your information is used only to operate our own messaging with you.

Message frequency and rates

Message frequency varies based on your appointments and interactions with us. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out

You can stop messages at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance.

Contact
